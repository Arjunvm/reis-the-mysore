Mysore Palace

Mysore Palace is one of the most magnificent buildings. It is a sight not to be missed when it is illuminated on Sundays and festive occasions.

Nandi Hills

Nandi Hills, or Nandidurg, is a hill fortress in the south Indian state of Karnataka.


KRS Dam

The dam site is bestowed with colorful, stunning and even illuminated gardens, musical fountains and water bodies.


Brindavan Gardens

Brindavan Gardens of Mysore is undeniably one of the best terrace gardens in the world. A must sightseeing place of a Mysore tour, Brindavan Garden mesmerizes everyone.


Balamuri Falls

Balmuri falls is a small water cascade located at Srirangapatna in Karnataka. Though known as a waterfall, it is a human-made check dam over the Cauvery river


Karanji Lake

Initially, this lake was built as a percolation tank by the Mysore King, to serve as source of drinking water for residents and was used for other chores like bathing, washing, etc.


Railway Museum

One of the major attractions of the museum is the Chamundi Gallery, which has an extensive range of pictures and paintings on the railways and its growth on display.
